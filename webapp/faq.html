FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • Is EventBloom free to use?

    Yes, browsing event packages and exploring general information is free. However, booking an event or consulting with an expert may involve a fee.

  • How do I create an account?

    Click the Sign Up button on the homepage, fill in the required details, and verify your email to activate your account.

  • Can I use EventBloom on mobile devices?

    Yes, our platform is mobile-friendly and can be accessed from smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

  • Can I customize my event?

    Yes! You can customize various aspects of your event, including venue setup, catering, decorations, and entertainment.

  • How far in advance should I book an event?

    We recommend booking at least one month in advance to ensure availability, especially for large-scale events.

  • Can I modify my booking after confirmation?

    Yes, but modifications must be made at least 7 days before the event. Contact customer support for assistance.

  • Can I cancel my event booking?

    Yes, cancellation policies vary based on the event type. Check our cancellation policy or contact support for details.

  • How can I contact an Event Planner?

    Registered users can communicate with an Event Planner through the platform's messaging system.

  • How do I schedule a consultation with an Event Moderator?

    Log in to your account, navigate to the Consultation section, select a moderator, and schedule an appointment. A small fee applies for expert consultation.

  • Can I contact the Event Moderator after booking?

    Yes, after booking an event, you can communicate with the assigned Event Moderator for updates and assistance.

  • What payment methods does EventBloom accept?

    We accept major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment methods.

  • Do I need to pay the full amount upfront?

    Some events may require a deposit, while others require full payment upon booking. The payment details will be provided during checkout.

  • How do I report a problem or give feedback about EventBloom?

    You can send feedback through the Contact Us page or leave a review in your account dashboard.
